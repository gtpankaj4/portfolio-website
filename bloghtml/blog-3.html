Exploring the New and Latest Visiting Places in Kathmandu Valley of Nepal

Published on April 12th, 2024 by Pankaj Bhatta

Kathmandu Valley has always been a hub for tourists and travelers alike. Known for its rich cultural heritage, ancient temples, and bustling marketplaces, the valley is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to immerse themselves in the unique blend of history and modernity that defines Nepal.

If you're planning a trip to Kathmandu Valley and looking for some new and exciting places to explore, then you're in luck. In recent years, the valley has witnessed a surge of new attractions that offer visitors a chance to experience the best of what the region has to offer. Here are some of the latest visiting places in Kathmandu Valley that you should add to your itinerary:

1) Kailashnath Mahadev Statue: Located in Sanga, Kavre, the Kailashnath Mahadev Statue is the tallest Shiva statue in the world. Standing at a height of 144 feet, the statue is a marvel of engineering and architecture and offers breathtaking views of the valley.

2) Chandragiri Hills: Situated on the outskirts of Kathmandu, Chandragiri Hills is a popular destination for hiking and trekking enthusiasts. With its lush green forests, scenic trails, and stunning views of the valley, the hills are a must-visit for anyone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

3) The Garden of Dreams: Located in the heart of Kathmandu, the Garden of Dreams is a serene oasis that offers visitors a chance to relax and unwind amidst beautiful gardens, fountains, and pavilions. The garden is a perfect place for a picnic or a leisurely stroll.

4) Bhaktapur Durbar Square: Bhaktapur Durbar Square is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that is home to some of the most well-preserved ancient temples and monuments in the valley. The square is a must-visit for anyone interested in history and architecture.

5) Boudhanath Stupa: One of the largest stupas in the world, the Boudhanath Stupa is a sacred pilgrimage site for Buddhists. Located on the outskirts of Kathmandu, the stupa is a must-visit for anyone looking to experience the spiritual side of Nepal.

These are just a few of the latest visiting places in Kathmandu Valley that you should add to your itinerary. Whether you're interested in history, culture, architecture, or nature, the valley has something to offer for everyone. So, pack your bags and get ready to explore the best of what Nepal has to offer.
